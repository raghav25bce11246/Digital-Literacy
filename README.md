### Task 4 — Email Etiquette & Digital Communication

**Part A — Professional Emails** (`task-4-email-etiquette.docx`)
Two fully formatted professional emails:
1. Student → Professor requesting an assignment deadline extension
2. Student → Internship Coordinator expressing interest in a summer internship

Both follow professional email rules: clear subject line, formal greeting, structured body, proper sign-off.

**Part B — Social Media Checklist** (`social-media-checklist.md`)
A Do's and Don'ts checklist with 7 Do's and 7 Don'ts for responsible social media use as a college student, including a quick-reference summary card.

**Project Report Reflection:**
Discussed the 2017 NHS WhatsApp miscommunication incident as a case study in how informal digital channels can cause serious real-world harm when used inappropriately in professional settings.
